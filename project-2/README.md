
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive sales dashboard for a global superstore to visualize sales performance by country, segment, category, and subcategory. Leveraging Power BI, the dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators and identify trends influenced by the shift to e-commerce during the pandemic. The solution supports data-driven decision-making by integrating multiple data sources into a cohesive analytical model.

---

## Business Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the shift from in-store to online shopping, creating challenges in understanding changing sales dynamics across regions and product categories. Retailers needed a unified view to analyze sales data effectively and respond to evolving customer behaviors. Without actionable insights, optimizing inventory, marketing, and sales strategies was difficult.

---

## Objective

- Create an interactive dashboard to visualize sales by country, segment, category, and subcategory
- Enable tracking of key sales KPIs and trends over time
- Support data-driven decision-making for retail stakeholders during shifting market conditions

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
- Power Query

---

## Project Workflow

- Gather and clean sales data from multiple sources
- Build a data model integrating country, segment, category, and subcategory dimensions
- Create key performance indicators using DAX calculations
- Design and develop interactive Power BI visuals including line and clustered column charts
- Publish and validate dashboard for actionable business insights

---

## Key Insights

- E-commerce sales increased significantly during the pandemic due to safety and convenience factors
- Sales performance varies widely across countries and product segments
- Certain categories and subcategories showed stronger growth trends aligned with consumer behavior shifts
- Visualizing sales data side-by-side by multiple dimensions enables more accurate business theories and solutions
