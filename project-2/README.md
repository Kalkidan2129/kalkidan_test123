
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive sales dashboard using Power BI to visualize and analyze sales data by country, segment, category, and subcategory. It enabled stakeholders to understand sales trends and make data-driven decisions during the shift to online shopping accelerated by the pandemic. The dashboard integrates multiple visualizations and KPIs to highlight key performance metrics effectively.

---

## Business Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic drastically changed consumer shopping behaviors, increasing the need for retailers to understand shifting sales patterns across different regions and product categories. Retailers required a centralized tool to monitor sales performance and adapt strategies quickly in an evolving digital marketplace. Without actionable insights, businesses risked losing competitive advantage and customer engagement.

---

## Objective

- Create an interactive dashboard to visualize sales by country, segment, category, and subcategory
- Enable tracking of key sales performance indicators and trends over time
- Facilitate data-driven decision making for retail management during market shifts

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel
- Power Query
- Data Modeling

---

## Project Workflow

- Gathered and prepared sales data from multiple sources
- Built data model and relationships in Power BI
- Designed and implemented key visualizations including line and clustered column charts
- Created KPIs and dynamic filters such as date slicers
- Developed final interactive dashboard for stakeholder use

---

## Key Insights

- Sales trends varied significantly by country during the pandemic period
- Certain product categories and subcategories outperformed others in online sales
- Customer segments showed distinct purchasing behaviors impacting sales distribution
- Monthly sales KPIs highlighted critical periods of growth and decline
