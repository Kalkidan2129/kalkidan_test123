
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze global superstore sales data by country, segment, category, and subcategory. It visualizes key sales metrics and trends to support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance and identify growth opportunities effectively.

---

## Business Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the shift to online shopping, changing consumer behavior and sales patterns globally. Retailers needed a comprehensive tool to understand these changes and optimize sales strategies across regions and product segments. Without clear insights, businesses struggled to adapt to evolving market demands and maximize revenue.

---

## Objective

- Design a dynamic dashboard to visualize sales data by multiple dimensions
- Enable tracking of key performance indicators (KPIs) such as sales trends and segment performance
- Support data-driven decisions to improve sales strategies during and post-pandemic

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data modeling
- Data visualization

---

## Project Workflow

- Collect and prepare global superstore sales data
- Build data model and relationships in Power BI
- Create KPIs and DAX calculations for sales metrics
- Design interactive visuals including line and clustered column charts
- Develop slicers and filters for dynamic data exploration

---

## Key Insights

- E-commerce sales increased significantly during pandemic lockdowns across multiple countries
- Certain product categories and segments outperformed others in different regions
- Sales trends varied month-to-month, highlighting seasonality and market shifts
- Visualizing sales by subcategory revealed niche opportunities for targeted marketing
