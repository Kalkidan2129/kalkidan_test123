
# Global Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project involved creating a comprehensive sales dashboard for a global superstore to visualize sales performance by country, segment, category, and subcategory. Leveraging Power BI, the dashboard integrates multiple visualizations and KPIs to support data-driven decisions. It highlights sales trends and customer preferences influenced by the shift to online shopping during the pandemic.

---

## Business Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic drastically changed consumer shopping behavior, increasing online sales and challenging retailers to quickly adapt. Retailers needed a unified view of sales data across regions and product lines to identify trends and optimize strategies. Without actionable insights, businesses risked losing competitive advantage in a rapidly evolving market.

---

## Objective

- Create a dynamic dashboard to track sales by country, segment, category, and subcategory
- Enable visualization of sales trends and key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Support decision-making through clear, actionable insights on retail performance

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## Project Workflow

- Gather and prepare sales data from multiple sources
- Build data model and relationships in Power BI
- Design and develop visualizations including line and clustered column charts
- Create KPIs and interactive filters like date slicers
- Publish and refine dashboard for actionable retail insights

---

## Key Insights

- Sales increased significantly in online channels during the pandemic period
- Certain product categories and subcategories outperformed others across regions
- Customer segments showed varying sales trends influenced by location and time
- Visualizing sales by multiple dimensions revealed hidden patterns and opportunities
